Mehmet Çolak

Artist Bio

Mehmet Çolak is an audio designer and interactive media artist from Turkey, currently based in Austria, studying his master's degree in Interface Cultures at University of Art and Design Linz. His design approach is to create soundscapes and interactive audio experiences with gamification and auditory environmental narration to enhance immersion.
